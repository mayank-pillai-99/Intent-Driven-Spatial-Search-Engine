Here are some fantastic sample queries you can use to test both the Spatial Database (DB) routing capabilities and the Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) capabilities of your application!

🎓 1. College (IIT Bombay) - DB Spatial Queries
These queries will test Qwen's ability to generate PostGIS spatial math (like ST_DWithin, ST_ClosestPoint, etc.) to route around a campus map.

Intent Search: "Find me a quiet place to study."
Intent Search: "Where can I get some cheap food."
Radius Search: "Find cafes within 500m of the library."
Nearest Neighbor: "Where is the nearest ATM to Hostel 12?"
📖 2. College (IIT Bombay) - RAG Queries
These queries test your RAG system's ability to parse the ugrulebook.pdf and fetch the exact academic regulations.

Attendance Rules: "What happens if my attendance is below 80%?"
Branch Change: "What CPI do I need for a branch change to LASE?"
Project Extensions: "How long of an extension can I get for my BTP?"


🛕 3. Golden Temple - DB Spatial Queries
These queries will test your engine's ability to map tourist locations, amenities, and historical sites in Amritsar.

Simple DB: "Find me the nearest toilet."
Simple DB: "Find restaurants where wheelchairs are allowed."
Intent Search: "Find me a quiet place"
Find me a cafe
👳 4. Golden Temple - RAG Queries
These queries test your RAG system's ability to pull hyper-specific tourist information and historical facts from the golden-temple-amritsar-travel-guide.pdf.

Simple RAG: "Where should I leave my shoes before entering?"
Simple RAG: "Am I allowed to take photos inside?"
Simple RAG: "What is the name of the holy tank?"
Temple Rules: "Can I swim or use soap in the Amrit Sarovar?"
